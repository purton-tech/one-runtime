Connect with One Runtime

Managed OAuth keeps setup lightweight. When your users follow a Gmail connection link, One Runtime will redirect them to Google for consent.

What your users will see

Expected consent copy

One Runtime wants access to your Gmail

This is normal when you choose managed OAuth. Google shows the OAuth app that is requesting access on behalf of your product.

One Runtime still enforces the scopes you configure and logs the resulting connection and token activity for your project.

Why teams choose managed OAuth

  • No Google app registration required to start evaluating Gmail access
  • Fastest path to testing connection links and agent actions
  • You can switch specific providers to BYO later for production

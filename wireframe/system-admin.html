Why this exists

One Runtime is the managed provider

Normal customers should not have to enter OAuth secrets for standard integrations. Internal operators keep those credentials here.

Separate internal responsibility

Agent builders browse integrations and call tools; system admins rotate secrets and resolve upstream credential failures.
